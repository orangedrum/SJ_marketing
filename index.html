How does this all work?

Unsigned Artists

Music posted on Soup Jam will come from our authentic and refreshing library of independant artists. We'll be updating our library frequently and will ensure the highest quality.

Local Businesses

Our sponsors are our muscle. Through a new and exciting way to advertise, they can promote themselves by sponsoring with us. It's because of them, you are feeding simply by listening.

1 Play | 1 Meal

We aim to enable you to feed the world by way of listening. By growing your musical library with us, you'll find a new side of your local community and feed a lot of someones.

Who's Behind This?

Soup Jam is the brain child of our founder, Jean Kaluza. Even after losing her ability to play drums, the urge to create and participate as a musician never went away. Transforming herself from professional musician to a Product Designer, Jean dreams of using her super powers to fuse communities together with the help of local musicians and interative design.
