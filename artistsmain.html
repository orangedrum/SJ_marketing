SoupJam Artists

Hone your craft. Get paid fairly. Feed the masses with your music.

What we look for

SoupJam is currently seeking local musicians of Central FL to help us populate our first 30 days of music! If you regularly play out, have a music video youtube, and tend to draw a crowd, you're our target! We're seeking all genres, styles and ages. We want a diverse pool and seeking to learn more about the local scene as we grow our base.

What you'll need to submit

In order to be considered, please read the following carefully and use the button to send us your submission. NOTE: It is important you own your own rights to your music and aren't subject to any legal or other restrictions. Good luck and we hope to someday feature you on SoupJam!

Please prepare to share with us the following:

  • Your band's name
  • A contact email we can reach out to you with more information
  • A link to a youtube music video of your band
  • Ability to verify your music is entirely original and you own all your music
